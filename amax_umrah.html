Hajj Packages

The fifth pillar of Islam — fulfilled with expertise, safety, and spiritual depth.

The Most Sacred Journey

Hajj is the pilgrimage to Mecca required of every Muslim at least once in their lifetime. At Amax Detroit, we handle every logistical detail so you can focus entirely on your spiritual journey. Our Hajj specialists have guided hundreds of Michigan families through this sacred obligation.

The Process

How We Guide You

01
Consultation
Meet our Hajj specialists in-person or virtually. We assess eligibility, financial planning, and group options.
02
Visa & Registration
We handle Hajj ministry registration, US passport authentication, and complete Saudi visa processing.
03
Pre-Departure Training
Mandatory sessions with certified scholars covering rituals, duas, and health & safety preparation.
04
Journey & Support
On-ground guides in Mecca, Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah — 24/7 emergency support throughout.
